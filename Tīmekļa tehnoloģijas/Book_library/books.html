Books are an important part of every educated person's life. For this reason, we should not think about such important things as literature in today's society where everyone spends half of their day on the Internet.

Here are quotes that inspire us to read a book:

  1. ‘Where books burn, soon people will burn.’ - Heinrich Heine.
  2. „Страницы книг — познания истоки,
    Но людям Жизнь в любые времена
    Преподавала главные уроки,
    Учительница лучшая — она.“     — Гамзат Цадаса
  3. ‘He who does not read good books has no advantage over a man who cannot read them.’ - Mark Twain.
  4. ‘A room without books is like a body without a soul.’ - Marcus Tullius Cicero.
  5. ‘Enlightened is the nation that lived, fought and built with a book in its hand.’ - Garegin Nzhdeh.
  6. ‘There are two motives for reading a book: one is that you like it; the other is that you can boast about it.’ - Bertrand Russell.
